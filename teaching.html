This page summarises teaching responsibilities within the Discipline of Civil & Environmental Engineering.

CIVE10006 Hydraulic Engineering IV

This is a 4th year undergraduate civil engineering module on unsteady flows delivered since the Spring semester of 2019. I am responsible for the delivery of material on open channel flows and specifically flood routing, St Venant Equations, Solitary Waves, Surges and Coastal Waves. Course Website

CIVE09035 Water Engineering, Transport & Treatment III

A 3rd year undergraduate civil engineering module covering the fundamentals of hydraulic flows since the Autumn semester of 2019. I am responsible for the delivery of material introducing the gradually varied flow equation, the use and rationale of certain flow-control structures, and the application of the momentum and energy principles on open channel flow settings. Course Website
