About us
--------

Welcome! These pages detail information on our activities within the Applied Fluid Mechanics Group, based at the University of Edinburgh. 

..  This site serves as a medium to summarise our progress and academic opportunities associated with our work on water research and environmental fluid mechanics.


Introduction
------------

.. figure:: support/profile.jpg
   :class: img-rounded
   :height: 200 px
   :align: left   


I am a *Lecturer* in Environmental Fluid Mechanics at the *University of Edinburgh*, incorporated within the Institute for Infrastructure and the Environment and leading the Applied Fluid Mechanics Group. In addition, as an Industrial Innovation  *Research Fellow* of the UK's Natural Environment Research Council (NERC) I coordinate research activities on computational methods for marine and offshore renewable energy, working closely with industry and other research groups.

.. I am a Chartered member of the Technical Chamber of Greece (TEE), a member of the Institution of Civil Engineers (ICE) and have been affiliated with the International Association of Hydro-environment Engineering and Research (IAHR). 

.. I have previously worked with the Department of Earth Science and Engineering at **Imperial College London** and the Hydro-environmental Research Centre of **Cardiff University**. 
.. More details are summarised in my `CV <_static/CV_2.pdf>`_.
